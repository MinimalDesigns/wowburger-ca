Why WOW?

When it comes to WOWBURGER, naturally the burgers are the star of the show! To make the best burgers, it starts with the very best ingredients. We spent months and months creating the perfect patty, made with three different cuts of the finest Irish beef for the perfect ratio of fat.

Our burger buns are specially made for us by Irish bakery Coghlan’s and our hand cut fries are chipped in house daily using the best of seasonal potatoes from Maris Pipers to Marquis Potatoes – depending on the season.
